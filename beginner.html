Body Blueprint® Beginner Guide

Welcome to your transformation. This guide is your foundation for lasting health, energy, and confidence.

Habit Formation & Mindset Architecture

  • Identity-based habits: Build from who you want to become, not just what you want to do.
  • Habit loop: Cue → Routine → Reward. Design your environment to trigger success.
  • Morning & evening rituals: Anchor your day with consistent, energizing routines.
  • Frictionless habit stacking: Link new habits to existing ones for effortless adoption.
  • Mindset shifts: Progress over perfection. Celebrate consistency, not intensity.

Nutrition Blueprint

  • Macronutrients: Prioritize protein (1g per lb of goal weight), moderate carbs, healthy fats.
  • Hydration: Aim for 2–3L water daily. Add electrolytes if training or sweating heavily.
  • Supplements: Optional: Creatine (5g/day), Vitamin D3 (2,000 IU), Magnesium (200–400mg).
  • Meal structure: Anchor each meal with protein, add fiber-rich carbs and healthy fats.
  • Rationale: Supports satiety, muscle retention, energy, and metabolic health.

FAQs & Troubleshooting

  • What if I miss a day? No problem. Resume the next day. Progress is built on consistency, not perfection.
  • Can I swap meals? Yes. Stay within the blueprint principles and prioritize protein.
  • What if I’m vegetarian? Use plant-based proteins like tofu, tempeh, legumes, and protein powders.
  • How do I track progress? Use the habit tracker, take weekly photos, and note energy, sleep, and mood.
  • Can I repeat this guide? Absolutely. Repetition builds mastery. You can cycle through again or move to Intermediary.
